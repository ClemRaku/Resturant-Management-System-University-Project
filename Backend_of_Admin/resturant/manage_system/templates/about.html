About Dhakaiya Bites

Founded in 2020, Dhakaiya Bites brings the authentic street flavors of Dhaka to your plate. From traditional family recipes to modern twists, we serve meals with love and passion.

Our mission is to delight every guest with fresh, flavorful dishes and impeccable service, making every visit a memorable culinary experience.
